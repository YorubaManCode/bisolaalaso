Welcome to Bisola Alaso about us page.

Bisola Alaso company has been in existence since the year 1954. We have sold clothes to both small and great. Most of the items we sell are yoruba attires. Please, go on our home page to see our selection. We love the yoruba culture. Bisola, the CEO, is married with children.
